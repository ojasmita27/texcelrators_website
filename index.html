About Texcelerators

Texcelerators is a vibrant tech club dedicated to fostering innovation, collaboration, and technical excellence. We bring together passionate individuals interested in robotics, machine learning, and emerging technologies.

Our mission is to provide a platform for students to learn, build, and innovate together. Through hackathons, workshops, and collaborative projects, we aim to accelerate technology adoption and create lasting impact in the tech community.
